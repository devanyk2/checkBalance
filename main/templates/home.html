1033 Program 

Program 1033 is a goverment initiative to funnel surplus military goods to police departments across the country, at a discount. This initiative has led to local security services acquiring armored vehicles, Weapons and other nonviolent military supplies. Use this service to find out what your local PD has acquired and how much was spent doing so.
